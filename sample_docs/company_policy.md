# Acme Corp — Employee Handbook & Policies (Sample)

This is a sample document for the internal-helpdesk demo. The DOC_SEARCH tool
retrieves from documents like this one to answer policy questions.

## 1. Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM, Monday through Friday, with a
30-minute unpaid lunch break. Core collaboration hours are 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM,
during which all employees are expected to be reachable.

## 2. Paid Time Off (PTO)
Full-time employees accrue **20 days** of paid vacation per year, plus **10**
public holidays. PTO accrues monthly and unused days carry over up to a maximum
of **5 days** into the next calendar year. Requests should be submitted at least
**two weeks** in advance through the HR portal.

## 3. Sick Leave
Employees receive **10 paid sick days** per year. A doctor's note is required
for absences longer than **3 consecutive days**.

## 4. Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week** with manager approval.
Fully remote arrangements require Director-level sign-off and are reviewed every
6 months.

## 5. Expense Reimbursement
Business expenses must be submitted within **30 days** of being incurred, with
itemized receipts. Meals are reimbursed up to **$50 per day** during travel.
Mileage is reimbursed at **$0.65 per mile**. Reimbursements are processed in the
next payroll cycle after approval.

## 6. Refund & Returns (Customer Policy)
Customers may return a product for a full refund within **30 days** of purchase,
provided the item is unused and in its original packaging. After 30 days, store
credit may be issued at the manager's discretion up to **60 days**. Shipping
costs are non-refundable.

## 7. Equipment
Each new employee receives a laptop and a one-time **$200** home-office
stipend. Equipment must be returned within **7 days** of leaving the company.

## 8. Code of Conduct
Harassment, discrimination, and sharing of confidential data are strictly
prohibited and may result in termination. Report concerns to HR or via the
anonymous ethics hotline.
